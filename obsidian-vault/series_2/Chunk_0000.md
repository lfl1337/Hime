## 🇯🇵 Source

> 体育館の二階、誰も来ない場所。安達はそこで島村と出会った。二人とも授業をサボっていた。それだけの関係だった——はずだった。

> The second floor of the gymnasium — a place no one ever came. That was where Adachi met Shimamura. Both of them were skipping class. That was all their relationship was supposed to be.
